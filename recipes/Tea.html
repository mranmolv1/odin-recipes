Tea

Ingredients:

  • Water
  • Tea leaves
  • Sugar

Steps:

- Place pan with 2 cups of water on burner and lit it.
- Add two spoons of sugar.
- When it starts to boil, add half spoon of tea leaves.
- After a while (almost 30 seconds-1 minute), add two cups of milk.
- Tea is ready. Filter it out and have 4 nice cups of Tea.
